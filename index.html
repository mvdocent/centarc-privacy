Privacy Policy

Effective date: 14 June 2026  ·  Last updated: 14 June 2026

Centarc is built on a single principle: your financial data belongs to you. We do not collect it, store it on our servers, or sell it. Ever.

What We Collect

Centarc stores all transaction data locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. If you have iCloud enabled, your data syncs via Apple's private CloudKit infrastructure — only accessible to your own Apple ID.

We do not have a backend database. We cannot access your transactions.

AI Category Detection

When you add a transaction manually or via voice, Centarc may send the merchant name to an AI service for category detection. Specifically:

  • Merchant names may be sent to Anthropic (Claude API) or Google (Gemini API) for categorisation
  • Transaction amounts and personal details are never sent to these services
  • Requests are stateless — the AI providers do not store or train on your queries under their API terms
  • Once a merchant is categorised, Centarc remembers it on-device so future lookups require no API call

You can view Anthropic's privacy policy at anthropic.com/privacy and Google's at policies.google.com/privacy.

Apple Pay Automation

Centarc uses an iOS Shortcuts automation to capture Apple Pay transactions. When triggered, the Shortcut passes the transaction amount and merchant name directly to the Centarc app on your device. This data:

  • Never leaves your device during the capture process
  • Is not sent to Centarc servers (we have none)
  • Is stored locally in SwiftData and optionally synced via your private iCloud

Analytics

Centarc uses TelemetryDeck for privacy-safe usage analytics. TelemetryDeck is designed to be GDPR-compliant and does not track individuals. Signals sent include app events such as "app launched" or "transaction added" — never financial data, merchant names, or amounts.

TelemetryDeck's privacy policy: telemetrydeck.com/privacy.

iCloud Sync

If you are signed into iCloud, Centarc syncs your data via Apple's CloudKit private database. This means:

  • Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest by Apple
  • Only devices signed into your Apple ID can access the data
  • Centarc has no access to your CloudKit data

You can disable iCloud sync for Centarc at any time in Settings → [your name] → iCloud → Centarc.

Data Retention & Deletion

All your data is stored on your device. To delete it:

  • Individual transactions: swipe left to delete in the app
  • All data: delete the Centarc app from your iPhone
  • iCloud copy: Settings → [your name] → iCloud → Manage Account Storage → Centarc → Delete Data

We hold no copies and cannot recover deleted data on your behalf.

Children's Privacy

Centarc is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Australian Privacy Act

Centarc is developed by an Australian-based individual and is subject to the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs). Because we do not collect personal information on our own servers, most APP obligations are met by design. For any privacy concerns or complaints, contact us using the details below.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the effective date above and notify users via an in-app notice. Continued use of Centarc after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
